Gender-Inclusive Restrooms

Map

Directions

Many of these directions include stairs. Ideal routes for people using wheelchairs may differ.

115 Greenough

1st Floor (along Welland)

Starting from the Cafeteria, walk toward Welland Road. Turn after the short flight of stairs and walk along the hallway. There will be an entrance way on your left between rooms 125 and 129. Walk through, and the bathroom will be directly in front of you. This bathroom is often closed for cleaning.

1st Floor (off Atrium)

Starting from the Atrium, walk between the columns and turn left before the MLK room. The gender-inclusive restroom is the furthest door to your right before the door outside.

2nd Floor (above Atrium)

Starting from the Atrium, climb up a flight of stairs and turn left. Walk a few meters. The bathroom will be on your right. This bathroom is between rooms 206 and 280, and is often closed toward the end of the day for cleaning.

2nd Floor (in auditorium)

Starting from the Overflow space, walk up the stairs to the 2nd floor then walk straight until the auditorium. If the doors are unlocked and no class is in session, enter the auditorium and walk onto the stage. Turn right immediately, then walk straight through a doorway. The bathroom should be in front of you. If class is in session or the front doors to the auditorium are locked, walk forward half a dozen meters and turn left. Walk along the hallway and down a short flight of stairs. Turn left, then take the next left. Be careful as there may be a class going on in the auditorium. Walk past two doors to the end of the hallway. The bathroom should be on your left.

3rd Floor

Starting from the Atrium, climb up a flight of stairs and turn right. Go up the staircase to your left. Turn right upon exiting the stair case and walk half a dozen meters. The bathroom will be on your right and through an entrance way. This bathroom is across from 388.

4th Floor

Starting from the Quad looking toward Welland Road, take the left staircase all the way to the fourth floor. There is a small hallway to your left, and the bathroom is located at the end. This bathroom is between 401A and 404.

UA Building

Basement

Entering the UA building from the front, walk down the stairs to your left. When you get to the bottom, turn right. Walk almost to the end of the hallway, until an empty doorway labeled 17 to your right. Then, walking through the doorway, proceed through the first doorway to your right.

22 Tappan

There are gender-inclusive restrooms on every floor of the 22 Tappan building.
