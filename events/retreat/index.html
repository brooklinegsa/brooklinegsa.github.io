GSA Retreat

Every year in the early fall, the GSA holds a weekend overnight field trip to a place in the woods. We sleep in cabins, doing a number of bonding activities and other fun things. Highlights include singing songs and roasting marshmallows. We have been unable to hold a retreat for the past two years due to the COVID-19 pandemic, but are hoping to hold it again in 2022!
