Trans Week of Awareness

Observed the 20th of November, the Transgender Day of Rememberance honors trans lives lost each year. The GSA usually holds a vigil, designs advisory lessons, makes posters, and builds a website. The GSA also celebrates Trans Week of Awareness as a way of highlighting the contributions of transgender people and celebrating the lives of those still with us.
